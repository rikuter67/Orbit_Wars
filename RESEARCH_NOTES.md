## Working Hypothesis

The strongest public heuristic family is not nearest-target sniping. It uses:

- Short-horizon future scoring.
- Safe source draining instead of emptying planets.
- Target value from production swing, capture cost, distance, pressure, and ETA.
- Greedy wave selection under source budgets.
- Regrouping idle ships toward useful frontlines.
- Separate behavior for 2-player and 4-player games.

## Current Local Implementation

`main.py` v1 implements a dependency-free approximation:

- Production/distance/cost target scoring.
- Simple enemy pressure estimate.
- Safe reserve per source planet.
- Reinforcement for visibly threatened owned planets.
- Moving-planet lead aiming from `initial_planets` and `angular_velocity`.
- Sun-crossing angle offsets.
- Late-game enemy pressure launches.
